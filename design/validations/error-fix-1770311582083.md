However, the transition post-login to the 'basic dashboard with mock content' is a critical juncture. Concerns primarily revolve around the lack of visual detail:
1.  **Predictability & Clarity:** How will the dashboard visually guide a new user? What 'mock content' is displayed, and does it clearly hint at future functionality or just appear as placeholders?

4.  **Explanation Timing:** For a foundational UI, most 'explanations' should be embedded in the UI itself. Without a UI, we cannot confirm if anything is implicitly explained too late.

The current design artifacts provide a strong conceptual foundation, but the lack of a visual layer (wireframes) prevents a comprehensive validation against the core rules, particularly regarding user predictability and UI-intent alignment.
